ingredients

Here is an ordered list of ingredients required in making one pack of spaghetti.

  1. 2 cooking spoon of vegetable oil
  2. 3 fingers of medium sized carrot
  3. green beans
  4. bell peppers(red, yellow, and green) 1 each
  5. 2 spring onions
  6. 5 large fresh tomatoes
  7. 6 habenero pepper
  8. 1 onion
  9. 2 stock cube
  10. 1/2 tablespoonful of mixed spices( mivina, all purpose seasoning, kitchen glory,1 tablespoonful of curry powder)
  11. 2 tablespoonful of dark soy sauce
  12. salt

There are many procedures and methods of cooking jollof spaghetti but i'll be using the stir fry method. After prepping ur ingredients like dicing the carrot, green beans, bell pepper, spring onion and the onion. I have also blended my fresh tomaotes and habenero pepper. Now let's start cooking

procedures

Here is an unordered procedures on how to prepare stir fry spaghetti

  • a pot of boiling water, add the spaghetti,
    some salt and some oil( to prevent it from sticking together), parboil for 10mins and sieve
  • Heat up the oil and fry the veggies ( carrot, green beans, bell peppers) for 3mins and scoop it out
  • Add a little oil to the pot, heat it up, saute√© the onion together with curry powder
  • Add the blended tomatoes and pepper,stir fry for 2mins
  • Add the mixed spices and stir fry till the tomatoes paste is almost dry
  • Pour in the washed spaghetti and stir
  • Add the stock cube and salt,
    stir together then add dark soy sauce,
    then the veggies and stir
  • Add the spring onion and stir to combine, cover for 2mins
  • Mix very well and serve with any protein of choice( chicken, egg, beef, fish)
